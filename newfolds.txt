newfold.md[1]
1. Context: What is Newfold Digital?

Newfold Digital is a global powerhouse in the web technology space, formed by the merger of Endurance International Group and Web.com Group. Unlike a traditional single-product company, Newfold operates as a "House of Brands."

The Mission: Helping small-to-medium businesses (SMBs) and entrepreneurs achieve success through a comprehensive digital presence.[1]

The Scale: 4,500+ employees across 30 office locations in 17 countries.[1]

The Strategy: Candidates often apply to "Newfold" but work for high-recognition sub-brands. Your redesign must bridge the gap between corporate stability (Newfold) and brand-specific passion (Bluehost, Yoast, etc.).

2. Brand Portfolio (The "House of Brands")

To effectively redesign the page, you must understand the primary brands under the Newfold umbrella:

Hosting/Presence: Bluehost, HostGator, Web.com, Domain.com.

Specialized Tools: Yoast (SEO), Network Solutions, Register.com.

E-commerce: SiteLock, SparkLine.

3. Current Career Page Architecture

The existing page (newfold.com/careers) uses a "top-down" funnel:

Hero: High-level mission statement ("Be a part of the fold").

Company Identity: Focus on SMB support and results-driven culture.

Global Presence: Highlighting the scale (17 countries, 30 offices).

DE&I & Culture: Heavy emphasis on "Authentic Selves" and Affinity Groups.

Employee Voices: Carousels of testimonials from global locations (Brazil, Ukraine, US, Indonesia).

CTA: Direct links to an external Workday ATS.

4. Cultural Pillars (Copywriting Context)

"Bring Your Authentic Self": This is their core diversity slogan. It emphasizes that employees don't need to fit a "corporate mold."

#TeamNewfold: The primary social media hashtag used for employer branding.

Affinity Groups: They currently promote over 20 unique groups (e.g., Black Professional Network, Women in Tech) to foster belonging.

Continuous Learning: Focus on global learning platforms and internal career growth/mentorship.

5. Visual & UX Audit for Redesign
Strengths to Keep:

Global Representation: The photography and testimonials feel truly international, not just US-centric.

Clear Value Prop: The focus on helping "small businesses" gives the work a sense of purpose beyond "tech for tech's sake."

Gaps to Address (Redesign Opportunities):

The "Workday" Leap: The transition from the polished marketing site to the Workday job board is a major UX drop-off. Consider designing a "Search" interface that feels native to the site.

Brand Clarity: Many candidates don't know which brands fall under Newfold. A "Find your brand" or interactive brand grid would improve candidate matching.

Benefits Specifics: Current copy mentions "Total Rewards" but lacks a clear, icon-based breakdown of actual perks (Remote work, Health, Equity, etc.).

Tech Stack Transparency: For engineering hires, there is very little information about the languages and tools used (PHP, React, Cloud Ops).

6. Redesign Content Components

Use these content blocks to build out your new layout:

A. The "Fold" Hero

Headline: Build the future of the internet, one small business at a time.

Sub-headline: Join a global team of 4,500+ innovators across 17 countries.

B. Global Impact Map

Visualizing the 30 offices (Jacksonville, Tempe, Austin, Mumbai, London, Rio, etc.).

C. The Brand Matrix

Logo wall or interactive hover-states for Bluehost, HostGator, Web.com, and Yoast.

D. Belonging & DEI

Highlight the 20+ Affinity Groups.

Messaging focus: "Diversity fuels our innovation."

7. Style Guide Notes

Voice: Professional, but warm and human. Avoid excessive "corporate-speak."

Color Palette: Use the Newfold Navy/Blue as a base, but allow sub-brand colors (HostGator Yellow, Bluehost Light Blue, Yoast Purple/Green) to appear as accents in the brand sections.

Typography: Bold, modern sans-serif for headlines; high-readability weights for body copy.
